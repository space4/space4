About Space4

Space4 is a co-working and events space in the heart of Finsbury Park, giving digital co-operatives and those working in positive sectors a space to meet, work and host events.

All profit generated from Space4 goes towards digital projects that make the world a fairer place.

Please note that unfortunately Space4 is not wheelchair accessible.

Desks

Space4 includes 25 desks, which can be used on a short- or long-term basis.

Space4 is not your run-of-the mill serviced coworking space. Instead it is run cooperatively, meaning that everyone working here shares responsibility for making it a success. This includes promoting the space and the people here, organising and attending events and maintaining it as a great place to work.
